# CoastFIRE Hub

Free FIRE calculator toolkit at [coastfirehub.com](https://coastfirehub.com).

## Tools

- **[Coast FIRE Calculator](https://coastfirehub.com/)** — Find your Coast number. 
  Supports couples mode and 3-scenario projections.
- **[Barista FIRE Calculator](https://coastfirehub.com/tools/barista-fire-calculator)** 
  — Calculate your Barista FIRE number.
- **[Fat FIRE Calculator](https://coastfirehub.com/tools/fat-fire-calculator)** 
  — Calculate your Fat FIRE number.
- **[FIRE Calculator](https://coastfirehub.com/tools/fire-calculator)** 
  — Universal FIRE calculator for all types.
- **[Coast FIRE Grid](https://coastfirehub.com/tools/coast-fire-grid)** 
  — Coast FIRE numbers by age, at a glance.

## Financial Assumptions

All calculators use consistent, source-backed assumptions:

| Parameter | Default | Source |
|-----------|---------|--------|
| Real return rate | 7% | S&P 500 historical avg minus 3% inflation |
| Safe Withdrawal Rate | 4% | Trinity Study (1998) |
| Inflation | 3% | US historical average |
| Retirement age (examples) | 65 | Gallup 2023 |
